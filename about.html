Nathalie Claudia Writer & Photographer

Hello,
I'm Nathalie.

Writer of small moments. Chaser of golden light.

I'm a writer and photographer based in Ja Ela, Sri Lanka. My work lives somewhere between personal essay and quiet journalism — I'm drawn to the texture of everyday life, to the stories that happen between appointments and in the margins of grander narratives.

I started writing seriously in my early twenties, mostly as a way to make sense of things. The camera came later — a secondhand film camera found at a market that I've since traded for digital, though I still think in film ratios and slow light.

What I write and photograph tends to be slow and close: rain on a window, the particular quiet of a library, the feeling of a city right before it wakes. I'm not trying to capture the extraordinary. I'm trying to show that ordinary things, held up to the light, are extraordinary enough.
